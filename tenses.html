Present

Present Tenses

1

Simple Present

Habits, facts, routines

S + V(s/es) + O
alwaysusually every dayoften
She reads books every night.
The sun rises in the east.
I don't like coffee.
Does he play football?
2

Present Continuous

Actions happening now

S + is/am/are + V-ing + O
nowat the moment currentlylook!
She is reading a book now.
They are playing outside.
I am not watching TV.
Is she studying?
3

Present Perfect

Past with present result

S + have/has + Past Participle
alreadyyet everjustsince/for
She has finished her homework.
I have never eaten sushi.
Have you visited Paris?
4

Present Perfect Continuous

Ongoing action until now

S + have/has + been + V-ing
forsince all daylately
She has been studying for 3 hours.
It has been raining all day.
He has been working since morning.
Past

Past Tenses

5

Simple Past

Completed actions in the past

S + V2 (Past form) + O
yesterdaylast week agoin 2010
She visited London last year.
They didn't come to the party.
Did you see that film?
6

Past Continuous

Ongoing action in the past

S + was/were + V-ing + O
whilewhen at 8 pm yesterday
She was sleeping when I arrived.
They were playing all evening.
Was he working at that time?
7

Past Perfect

Before another past event

S + had + Past Participle
beforeafter alreadyby the time
She had left before I arrived.
They had finished dinner already.
Had you met him before?
8

Past Perfect Continuous

Ongoing action before a past point

S + had + been + V-ing
forsince beforewhen
She had been waiting for an hour.
He had been working all night.
They had been arguing before I came.
Future

Future Tenses

9

Simple Future

Decisions, predictions, promises

S + will + Base Verb
tomorrownext week soonin the future
She will call you tomorrow.
I won't be late.
Will you come to the party?
10

Future Continuous

Ongoing action at a future time

S + will be + V-ing
at this time tomorrowat 8 pm tonight
She will be sleeping at midnight.
They will be working all day.
Will you be joining us?
11

Future Perfect

Completed before a future point

S + will have + Past Participle
by tomorrowby 2026 before
She will have finished by noon.
They will have left before you arrive.
12

Future Perfect Continuous

Ongoing until a future point

S + will have been + V-ing
forby the time
By May, she will have been studying for 3 years.
He will have been working for 10 hours by sunset.

6 Past Continuous S + was/were + V1-ing while, when
7 Past Perfect S + had + V3 before, after, by the time
8 Past Perfect Continuous S + had been + V1-ing for, since (in the past)

10 Future Continuous S + will be + V1-ing at this time tomorrow
11 Future Perfect S + will have + V3 by tomorrow, before
12 Future Perfect Continuous S + will have been + V1-ing for, by the time
