Tenses

The 12 English Tenses

Quick overview — click any card for a full lesson.

Simple Present

Habits & facts

She works every day.

Present Continuous

Actions happening now

She is working now.

Present Perfect

Past with present effect

She has worked here.

Simple Past

Completed past actions

She worked yesterday.

Past Continuous

Ongoing past actions

She was working.

Past Perfect

Before another past event

She had worked.
