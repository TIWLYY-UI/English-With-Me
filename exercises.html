Fill in the Blank

Type the missing word in each sentence.

1. She ______ to school by bus every day.
Hint: Simple Present — subject is "She"
✅ Answer: goes
2. They ______ football when it started to rain.
Hint: Past Continuous
✅ Answer: were playing
3. I have ______ been to Japan.
Hint: Present Perfect — frequency adverb (negative)
✅ Answer: never
4. By the time he arrived, she ______.
Hint: Past Perfect
✅ Answer: had left
5. If I ______ rich, I would travel the world.
Hint: Second Conditional — use past tense of "be"
✅ Answer: were
6. The letter ______ by Tom last night.
Hint: Passive Voice — Simple Past
✅ Answer: was written
